Everything you need to know about Buba Junction.

Bubble tea, also known as Boba tea, is a Taiwanese drink that combines tea, milk, and chewy tapioca pearls. It's a fun, customizable beverage loved worldwide for its unique texture and delicious flavors.
Our Brown Sugar Boba Milk Tea and Classic Milk Tea are customer favorites. The Mango Fruit Tea, Taro Boba, and Cold Coffee series are also highly recommended by our regulars.
